Player Insurance & Injury Claims

Important information about your insurance coverage and the claims process

Your Insurance Coverage

All registered Footscray Rugby Club players are covered by Rugby Australia's insurance policy. This coverage is included in your annual membership fees and provides protection for injuries sustained during:

  • Official club training sessions
  • Sanctioned matches and games
  • Club-approved rugby activities

Non-Medicare Medical Expenses

Loss of Income

$300 per week for 52 weeks

Capped at 100% of your average weekly income from the 12 months prior to injury

28-day deferral period applies

Requirements for Loss of Income Claims:
  • Employment Declaration: Your employer must complete employment details, or provide proof of earnings if self-employed
  • Tax File Number: Must submit TFN Declaration form to secure email
  • Medical Certificates: Required every 4 weeks - no backdated certificates accepted
  • Return to Work: Must provide clearance certificate when returning to work
How Payments Work:
  • Deferral Period: No payment for first 28 days off work
  • Maximum Duration: Up to 52 weeks of payments
  • Income Cap: Cannot exceed your actual weekly earnings
  • Ongoing Requirement: Must remain under regular medical care

Ambulance Services

100% coverage

Emergency transport expenses with no excess

Important Conditions

  • Medicare Exclusion: No coverage for expenses that Medicare covers (prohibited by law)
  • Time Limits: Medical expenses must be claimed within 12 months of injury
  • Doctor Referral Required: Referrals must be obtained BEFORE treatment begins
  • Private Health First: You must claim from private health insurance before submitting to SLE
  • Medical Certificates: Required every 4 weeks for loss of income claims (no backdated certificates)
